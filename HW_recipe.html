Banana Bread Recipe

This is a simple and easy guide for making delicious Banana Bread

Ingredients Deguired

  • 140g of softened butter
  • 140g of caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 140g of self-raising flourr
  • 1 tsp of baking poweder
  • 2 ripe banans
  • 50g of icing sugar
  • a handful of dried banana chips for deceration

Steps to Baking

  1. Heat oven to 180C
  2. Butter a 2lb loaf tin (line the base/sides with baking parchment)
  3. Cream 140g softened butter and 140g caster sugar until light and fluffy
  4. slowly add 2 beaten large eggs with a little of the 140g flour
  5. Fold in remaining flour, 1 tsp baking powder and 2 mashed bananas
  6. Pour the mixture into the prepared tin and bake for 50 mins
  7. Cool in the tin for 10 mins, then remove to a wire rack
  8. Mix 50g icing sugar with 2-3 tsp water to make a runny icing
  9. Drizzle the icing across the top of the cake and decorate with a handful of banana chips
